IF I STAY

DESCRIPTION

If I Stay is a young adult novel[1] by Gayle Forman published in 2009. 
            The story follows 17-year-old Mia Hall as she deals with
            the aftermath of a catastrophic car accident involving her family..
            Mia finds herself stuck between two worlds:
            the world of the living, and the world of those who have moved on.
